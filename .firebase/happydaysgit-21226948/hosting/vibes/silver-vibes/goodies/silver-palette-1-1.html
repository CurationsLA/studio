### Enhanced Context-Specific Applications

Web Applications:

  • Hero Sections: Purple gradient background with white text
  • Headers: Blue backgrounds or universal background with blue navigation
  • Content Areas: Universal background (`#fafbfc`) with trio accents
  • CTAs: Green buttons with dark text on universal background
  • Notifications: Pink badges on universal background
  • Forms: Blue for focus states, green for validation on universal background

Mobile Apps:

  • Splash Screen: Purple gradient hero style
  • Main Background: Universal background (`#fafbfc`) throughout
  • Tab bars: Blue for active states on universal background
  • Action buttons: Green for primary actions
  • Badges: Pink for notifications and new features
  • Success states: Green checkmarks and confirmations

Marketing Materials:

  • Hero Banners: Purple gradient with white text
  • Background: Universal (`#fafbfc`) for all content sections
  • Headlines: Blue for trust-building statements
  • CTAs: Green for "Sign Up", "Get Started", "Buy Now"
  • Accents: Pink for special offers and limited-time promotions
  • Cards: White cards with subtle shadows on universal background
